About the Club

Bengaluru's Most Passionate Football Community

Founded in 2012, Ronaldo FC Bengaluru started on the turfs of Jayanagar with a single vision — to give every aspiring footballer in Bengaluru a home. What began as a weekend kick-about has grown into one of Karnataka's most respected football clubs.

We are more than a club. We are a brotherhood forged on the pitch, a community that believes football has the power to transform lives, build discipline, and create champions — on and off the field.

🎯
Vision

To produce nationally competitive players from grassroots Bengaluru football.

⚡
Mission

Elite coaching, structured development pathways, and a winning culture for every age group.

🤝
Values

Discipline, respect, teamwork, and relentless pursuit of excellence on and off the field.
